Our Mission

  • Cost-saving: Ride-sharing can be a great way for university students to save money on transportation costs. By sharing the cost of fuel and vehicle maintenance with other students, students can reduce their transportation expenses significantly.
  • Environmental benefits: Ride-sharing can help reduce the number of cars on the road, thereby reducing carbon emissions and contributing to a cleaner environment.
  • Time-saving: Sharing a ride can also save time, as students can take turns driving and avoid having to navigate traffic and search for parking spots. This can be particularly important for students who have a long commute or who have to travel across the city to get to classes.
  • Socializing: Carpooling can also provide opportunities for socializing and building connections with other students. This can be especially beneficial for new students who are still getting to know people on campus.
  • Increased accessibility: For students who don't have access to a car, ride-sharing can provide a means of transportation that would otherwise be unavailable to them. This can help ensure that all students have equal access to the resources and opportunities available on campus.
